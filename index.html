Who is Telemedica LLC?

Mission:

Our mission is to continually innovate quality care for the Veteran Community through support, compassion, and a tech-forward approach. We are committed to serving those who served.

Vision:

To be the most trusted health resource that connects the Veteran Community to a network of care providers on their path to wellbeing.

Purpose:

To provide ease and accessibility to world-class care providers for veterans seeking to improve their quality of life.

What We Do



IMOs & DBQs

Evidence to support your VA claim. Nexus Letters (IMOs) and DBQs do not require an appointment. Simply book, upload your documents, and one of our licensed professionals will be in touch to let you know if a medical document is warranted.



Mental & Medical Evaluations

Receive a mental health or medical evaluation from one of our trusted, licensed providers. We conduct evaluations of 21 physical conditions, and can help you identify mental health condition(s) you may be living with.



Recurring Therapy

Ongoing therapy tailored to the Veteran Client experience. Receive mental health care from the comfort of your own home through our convenient and secure HIPAA-Compliant telehealth platform.
